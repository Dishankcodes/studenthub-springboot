Our Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 January 2026

At Student Hub, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how your data is collected, used, and safeguarded while using our learning and career guidance services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name and email address
  • Login credentials (securely encrypted)
  • Course enrollments, progress, and certificates
  • Career guidance forms and mentorship requests
  • Device and usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To manage your account and platform access
  • To personalize learning recommendations
  • To provide mentorship and career support
  • To improve platform performance and security

3. Data Protection

  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure servers and SSL protection
  • Restricted access to sensitive data

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information is shared only with trusted services when required for payments or legal obligations.

5. Cookies

Cookies help us enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and provide relevant content. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Access or update your personal data
  • Request account deletion
  • Opt out of promotional communication

7. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact us at support@studenthub.com

This privacy policy is created for educational and project demonstration purposes.
