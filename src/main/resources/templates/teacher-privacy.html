Teacher Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: January 2026

EduPlatform respects the privacy of all teachers, instructors, and educators. This policy explains how your data is collected, used, and safeguarded.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: Name, Email, Phone (optional)
  • Professional info: Qualifications, Profile, Skills
  • Uploaded content: Videos, Notes, Assignments
  • Payment details: Earnings, Withdrawals (if monetized)
  • Analytics: Engagement, ratings, performance
  • Security & device info: Login logs, IP address

2. How We Use Teacher Data

  • Manage accounts and course content
  • Display instructor profile to students
  • Track engagement and performance
  • Process course payments if applicable
  • Respond to support & feedback

3. Content Ownership

Teachers retain ownership of their content but grant EduPlatform permission to host, display, and deliver it to students.

4. Data Protection

  • Encrypted storage of sensitive info
  • Secure payment and SSL encryption
  • Restricted access to confidential data

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell teacher data. Sharing occurs only when required for payments, legal compliance, or authorized services.

6. Rights of Teachers

  • Request data correction
  • Request account or data deletion
  • Update teaching profile anytime

7. Policy Updates

Changes will be reflected on this page with the updated revision date.

8. Contact Support

For privacy concerns: teacher-support@eduplatform.com

This privacy policy is for demonstration purposes. Legal review recommended before commercial launch.
